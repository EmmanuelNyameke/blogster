Suspected Thief Beaten To Pulp at Bibiani

A tragic incident that has happened at Bibiani in the Western North Region of Ghana indicates that a suspected thief has been beaten to pulp.

According to the reporter of Angel Broadcasting Network (ABN), Kofi Gyabaah, the suspected thief use to help both the traders and buyers with their goods but bolt away whenever the civilians around that vicinity are low in number.

Unfortunately, luck eluded his side when he attempted to bolt away with a sack of cassava.

The traders beat him up mercilessly and the suspected thief who is believed to be in his early thirties (30s) sustained injuries.

This incident happened on Friday, April 12, 2024.
