American Rapper To Cut Sod For an Anglican School

The American rapper, Jeffrey Bruce Atkins but popularly known as Jah Rule has planned to arrive in Ghana on Wednesday, April 10, 2024 to cut sod. Jah Rule has adapted a school at Manya Krobo in the Eastern Region of Ghana. The name of the school is Nduaso Anglican School and is in the Lower Manya Krobo Municipal District.

It is known that Jah Rule will return to Ghana on Wednesday evening and on Thursday, April 11, 2024, he will set off to Manya Krobo to cut sod for the openning of Nduaso Anglican School. This was revealed on Monday, April 8, 2024 by an entertainment pundit, Nene on Neat FM.
